Update: Merapi, Wasior, Mentawai Appeal extended in response to latest eruption
Post: November 11th, 2010
Mount Merapi erupted yet again on November 5, 2010, spewing blistering gas 6 miles into the sky and unleashing a pyroclastic flow that torched everything in its path. Volcanic ash rained down on towns up to 150 miles away.
Wasior, Mentawai, and Merapi Disasters Urgent Appeal
Post: October 28th, 2013
Three deadly natural disasters hit Indonesia in the month of October 2010. On October 4, a flash flood ravaged Wasior village in West Papua. The tsunami-like waters over 3 feet high washed away homes, leaving 144 dead, 123 missing and thousands displaced and injured.
